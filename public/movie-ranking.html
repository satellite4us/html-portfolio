The Best Movies According to David

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Grown Ups & Grown Ups 2 Duology

Funny adventures of five good friends and former teammates reuniting.

John Wick Tetralogy

A cool story about a legendary hitman who is reluctantly drawn back into the criminal underworld after retiring.

Old School

A hilarious movie of three friends attempting to recapture their glory days by opening up a fraternity near their alma mater.
